My Journey

Melkamu Kebede – I’m a recent student at AASTU (Addis Ababa Science and Technology University), where I levelled up my academic journey after scoring 519.19 on the entrance exam. I spend most of my time building new technology skills, deepening my knowledge in science, technology, and innovation, shaping my passion for problem-solving and creativity.

Early Passion for Innovation — Since childhood, I’ve been fascinated by solving tech-based problems in my community. By grade six, I found my second home in an innovation center, experimenting and creating solutions for real-world challenges. Despite my dedication to innovation, I maintained top academic performance, which eventually led me to Hayole Educational Project School in Hawasa, Sidama Regional State.

Achievements in Technology & Software Development — At Hayole, I developed fully functional software projects to address various challenges, focusing on Android (Java) and web development (JavaScript, HTML, CSS). Currently, at AASTU Innovation Center, I am completing two advanced Android applications designed to solve governmental and educational inefficiencies — bridging technology with Ethiopia's real needs.

My journey from a curious child fixing gadgets to a software developer creating impactful solutions reflects my unwavering commitment to innovation. With the right opportunities, I aim to push boundaries in technology and beyond for my community and country.
